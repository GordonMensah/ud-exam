Fix forward-window bleed, heading-sentence concat, vague teaching points

Four targeted fixes for wrong commentary and options:

1. Forward window 400→150 chars (Step 5)
   In this book, the verse is CITED AFTER the teaching statement, so
   relevant content is mostly BEFORE the ref. A 400-char forward
   window bled past section headings (e.g. "Can Rebels Repent?") into
   completely unrelated text.
   Fix: 1 Timothy 3:15 no longer picks up "Every single disciple,
   except John the beloved..." from the next section.

2. Commentary loop now uses finditer (same as topics loop)
   The old _SENTENCE_RE.split() approach discarded delimiters, so
   rhetorical questions ("Is this not repentance?") and short
   exclamatory headings ("Friendly and Flashy!") were indistinguishable
   from normal statements.  Both loops now:
     - skip ?-ending sentences (rhetorical questions)
     - skip !-ending sentences with ≤5 words (exclamatory headings)

3. _HEADING_CONCAT_RE: truncate numbered-point headings at body join
   After whitespace normalization, "7. In order to reap our full reward"
   and its first body sentence "Those who benefit..." run together with
   no punctuation between them.  The regex detects the join point
   ([a-z] Those/These/They/Very/There/The/I) and truncates to the
   clean heading only:
     "In order to reap our full reward Those who benefit..."
       → "In order to reap our full reward"
     "To fight the 5th column Very early in my ministry..."
       → "To fight the 5th column"
     "For the love of God to fill the church The ministry..."
       → "For the love of God to fill the church"

4. _VAGUE_DEMON_RE: filter vague demonstrative teaching sentences
   "Do not make that mistake" / "Avoid this error" require prior
   context to be meaningful.  Added to _good_text() and to
   teaching_points extraction.
