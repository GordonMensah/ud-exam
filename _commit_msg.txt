Filter rhetorical questions from topics extraction

Root cause: topics split on [.;!?] consumed the "?" delimiter,
making rhetorical questions like "Is this not repentance?" and
"Didn't he repent?" indistinguishable from normal topic phrases.

Fix: changed topics extraction to use re.finditer() so the
trailing delimiter is preserved, and question-ending sentences
(delimiter == '?') are skipped entirely.

Also expanded _META_PREFIXES with:
  "i have often", "i have wondered", "i have known",
  "what about", "why is", "this is why"

Result (Matthew 27:5 topics):
  Before: ['What about Judas', 'Didn't he repent',
           'Is this not repentance', ...]
  After:  ['To repent means to turn around, and to change',
           'Judas never changed']

All 180 questions regenerated (seed 42).
